Environmental Factor Ranges

Here are the ranges for various environmental factors:

Nitrogen content should be between 0 to 140 kg/ha.

Phosphorus content should range from 5 to 128 ppm.

Potassium content should be between 5 to 92.5 mg/kg.

Temperature should vary from 14°C to 37.2°C.

Humidity should be in the range of 15.7% to 99.9%.

pH levels should be between 4.5 to 8.3.

Rainfall should range from 0 to 213 mm.

Soil Types and Recommended Crops

Red Soil: Red soil is suitable for crops like Cotton, Wheat, Pulses, Millets, Oilseeds, Chickpea, Pigeonpeas, Mothbeans, Mungbean, Blackgram, Jute, Banana, Apple.

Alluvial Soil: Alluvial soil is fertile and suitable for crops like Rice, Sugarcane, Maize, Cotton, Banana, Mango, Orange, Papaya, Coffee, Kidneybeans, Pomegranate.

Black Soil: Black soil (also known as Regur soil) is ideal for crops like Cotton, Millets, Sugarcane, Soybeans, Wheat, Jute, Kidneybeans, Chickpea, Pomegranate.

Saline Soil: Saline soil is suitable for crops like Sugar beet, Cotton, Pigeonpeas, Kidneybeans.
