Crop Recommendation System

About the Project

This Crop Recommendation System is developed to assist farmers in selecting the best crops for their fields based on various factors. Using the power of machine learning, specifically the Random Forest algorithm, the system analyzes environmental and soil parameters to provide accurate crop recommendations.
